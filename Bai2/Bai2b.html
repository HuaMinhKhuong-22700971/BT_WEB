Apple Bavarian Torte (★★★★☆)

A classic European torte baked in a spring form pan Cream cheese, sliced almonds, and apple make this the perfect holiday treat (12 servings).

INGREDIENTS

  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 1/3 cup white sugar
  • 1/4 teaspoon canilla extract
  • 1 cap all_purpose flour
  • 1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese
  • 1/4 cup white sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 1/2 teaspoon canilla extract
  • 6 apples - peeled, cored, and sliced
  • 1/3 cup white sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon fround cinnamon
  • 1/4 cup sliced almonds

DIRECTIONS

  1. Preheat oven to 450 degrees F (230 degrees C).
  2. Cream together butter, sugar, vanilla, and flour.
  3. Press dough into the bottom of a 9 inch springform pan. Set aside.
  4. In a medium bowl, blend cream cheese and sugar. Beat in egg and vanilla. Pour cheese mixture over crust.
  5. Toss apples with sugar and cinnamon. Spread apple mixture over cream cheese layer. Sprinkle with almonds.
  6. Bake for 10 minutes. Reduce heat to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C) and continue baking for 25 minutes.
  7. Sprinkle almonds over top of torte. Continue baking until lightly browned. Cool before removing from pan.
